About TranslationZone.com

The Source for Translation Professionals

TranslationZone is a free service provided by TRADOS, THE LANGUAGE ARCHITECTS. Launched in the Spring of 2000, TranslationZone is becoming the premier online community for translation professionals. We offer:
  • A place for professional translators to showcase their skills, experience and expertise.
  • A growing directory for translation agencies and clients to find TRADOS-savvy freelance translators.
  • Valuable translation industry insights, news and event listings all in one place.
  • Online discussion forums and live chat rooms for exchanging views, professional tips and cooperative support.


About TRADOS

TRADOS is the recognized leader in translation memory solutions. TRADOS deploys state-of-the-art linguistic database technologies to support, accelerate, enhance, and streamline the human translation process. The TRADOS solution, available in Team and Freelance editions, comprises a suite of complementary applications engineered to boost the efficiency and quality of multilingual communication. Founded in 1984, TRADOS has operations worldwide. To learn more, visit http://www.trados.com/.
