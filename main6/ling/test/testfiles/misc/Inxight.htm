LinguistX Platform

The LinguistX Platform is a fast, comprehensive suite of multilingual text services. It includes automatic language identification, tokenization, stemming, part-of-speech tagging, and noun/phrase extraction. It is based on flexible, high-performance, finite-state technology invented at Xerox Palo Alto Research Center (Xerox PARC), and powers many of the web's premier search engines.

For more information about LinguistX Platform, please download the LinguistX Platform data sheet (LinguistX Platform data sheet). To learn more about using linguistic technology for business applications, please download the LinquisticX Platform White Paper (Distilling Information From Documents).

As with all Inxight products, you'll have the support you need to apply this advanced technology to your web building projects (LinguistX Platform Support).
