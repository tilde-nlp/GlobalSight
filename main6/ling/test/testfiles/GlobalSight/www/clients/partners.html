RWS Polyglot
RWS-Polyglot is the second largest translation and localization company in the world and one of but two translation services firms in the US to have achieved ISO 9002 accreditation. Headquartered in San Francisco and with access to over 1000 professional translators via its 7 offices around the world, RWS-Polyglot has the resources and expertise to handle the most demanding translation and localization projects. As partners, Global Sight and Polyglot provide cost effective, market driven, multilingual web solutions for multilnational companies. Polyglot is well versed in the usage of Global Sight Ambassador, and is prepared, along with Global Sight, to take your web site to the rest of the world.
 
Interwoven
Interwoven, Inc. is the leading provider of open systems for Enterprise Web Production (EWP). The company's flagship product is designed for large, complex internet and intranet web operations maintained by multiple development teams and potentially thousands of content contributors distributed throughout the enterprise. As enterprises deploy large-scale multilingual Web sites, they increasingly look to a combination of best-of-breed solutions to address their application requirements. Working together, Global Sight and Interwoven provide a powerful solution for building, deploying and maintaining mission-critical global Web sites. The two companies cooperate in jointly promoting Global Sight Ambassador and Interwoven Team-Site.
 
UUNET
UUNET, an MCI WorldCom Company, formerly UUNET Technologies, Inc., is the first commercial Internet Service Provider (ISP) and a global leader in Internet communications solutions for businesses, online service providers and telecommunications firms around the world. UUNET is one of the only ISPs to offer multinational customers 'Global Hosting' services by which a multinational can host its web site at multiple locations around the world through one point of contact and with one bill. Global Sight and UUNET cooperate in jointly promoting GlobalSight Ambassador and UUNET's Global Hosting services to help fulfill the international Web needs of multinationals.
