Jorden Woods, co-founder and CEO of Global Sight, is a pioneer in the multilingual Web field. Recognized as a leading authority on Web globalization issues, Jorden has been invited to speak at numerous conferences including Internet World (Spring and Summer 98), World Education's Global Workforce Conference (1998), and the IQPC Global Web Site Localization conferences (1998, 1999)

Jorden brings an in-depth understanding of global markets, cultures and languages from having lived and worked for over eight years in Asia and Europe. Prior to co-founding Global Sight, he was the Director of information technology at ERM Hong Kong, a multinational environmental consulting firm. Jorden helped to transform the organization at ERM from a local office of 25 people to a regional hub that supported the Asia Pacific market with over 100 employees.

Jorden holds a B.S. degree in astrophysics and a master's degree in physics from the California Institute of Technology (Caltech). In addition to his native English, Jorden's foreign language expertise includes Chinese, Korean, Japanese and French. An avid cycling enthusiast, he has made cross-country bike journeys across the US, South Korea, Taiwan, Japan, and France.

 
A co-founder of Global Sight, Excelle has a rich multicultural background. She brings more than six years of international marketing management experience and three years of foreign diplomacy experience to the Global Sight team.

A native of China, Excelle began her career at the Chinese Foreign Affairs offices. Her diplomatic career provided the opportunity to work with a variety of foreign dignitaries and government officials from the US and UK, including Prince Phillip during his historic visit to PRC on behalf of the World Wildlife Foundation.

After earning her masters of international business in the US at Thunderbird in 1992, Excelle joined Scott Worldwide as regional marketing director, based in Hong Kong. During her six-year tenure at Scott, Excelle successfully spearheaded the company's regional product launches and managed marketing operations throughout the Asia-Pacific territory.

 
David Ron has 20 years of senior financial management experience in diverse corporate environments, including startup technology companies and large multinationals, such as TV Interactive Corp. (TVI), IBM Corporation's Metaphor subsidiary and McCune Sound Services.

Prior to Global Sight, David was the CFO at TVI, where he managed the financial operations including financing activities, contracts, treasury, performance measurements and forecasting. At TVI, he closed four financing rounds, including strategic partnerships with companies such as Intel and NTT. Mr. Ron graduated cum laude from Boston University with a bachelor's degree in science/business management, and from Suffolk University with a master's degree in business administration.

 
 
Bill Franklin has broad range of experience in software engineering, quality assurance, internet technology and project management. John Carr brings over 9 years of global marketing, sales and business development experience to the Global Sight team.
