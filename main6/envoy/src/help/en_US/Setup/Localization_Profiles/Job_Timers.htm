Job Timers

Summary:

If a Job has not met word count minimum, it will be moved to Ready or In Progress in the time frame set here.

Only used when by Word Count option is chosen.  

No Timers…

Jobs remain in Pending until word count minimum is met

Every…

Jobs are moved every x days/minutes/hours starting at the listed time

Example:  Every 90 minutes starting at 8am

At…

Jobs are moved at the selected time on the selected days

Example: every Wednesday and Friday at 8pm
