Looping Workflow with Finish Options

The workflow  below is the same as the Looping Workflow above except that it also gives John Smith, in the Edit stage, one extra option for forwarding the workflow after he is finished -- a QA stage. So after John Smith finishes his Edit activity, he will have the choice of sending the workflow :

  1. Back to Translation

  2. To QA , which will then go to Final Review

  3. Directly to Final Review
