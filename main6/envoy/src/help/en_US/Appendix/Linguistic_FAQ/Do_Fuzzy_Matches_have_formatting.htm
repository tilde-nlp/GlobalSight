Do Fuzzy Matches have formatting

Yes. Fuzzy Matches are returned with as much formatting as is allowed by the match score. If it's a high score, for example a Demoted Exact Match, it will contain all formatting of the original, but if it's a low match score, it may contain very little formatting. If formatting is missing, the Localization Participant must re-insert the formatting using placeholder tags based on the source segment.

 

Exact Matches, including Demoted Exact Matches, are returned with all formatting. Exact Matches are also copied into the target, so no further manipulation is required by the Localization Participant.
