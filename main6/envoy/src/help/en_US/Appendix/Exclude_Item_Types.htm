Exclude Item Types

Exclude Item Types are types of items (segments) that you can exclude from the translation process. Using Exclude Item Types can make translation much simpler and faster because translator are not bothered with extraneous data. When items are excluded, that means they will not be editable in the online editor and will not be included in the offline translation file.

 

Enter Exclude Item Types into the Exclude Item Types box on the Localization Profile Screen. Each item must be delimited with a pipe "|".

 

The complete list of items that you can exclude is listed under the Complete List heading below. For your convenience we've also provided two commonly used collections of Exclude Item Types - a short list and a long list. Just copy and paste these lists into the Exclude Item Types box on the Localization Profile screen (remove line breaks) and then import files.
