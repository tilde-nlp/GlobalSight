Overview - Workflow Manager

Getting Started

Click on the My Activities link in the toolbar above the word "Home" to get started.

 

Click the Setup link to access Calendars/Holidays and Support Files.

 

Click My Jobs link to access Pending, Ready, In Progress, Exported and Archived workflows.

Menus

Menus for accessing My Jobs and My Activities.

These menus contain links to display all jobs or activities in a particular state.  Once a search has been performed, the menu contains a link to the most recent search.  These menus also contain links to the three most recently accessed jobs and activities.
