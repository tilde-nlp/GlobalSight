Contact Information

Summary

Change your contact information.

Address:

Your address may be used by your project manager to send you physical mail.

Home Phone:

Your home phone may be used by your project manager to contact you after business hours.

Work Phone:

Your work phone may be used by your project manager to contact you during business hours.

Cell Phone:

Your cell phone may be used by your project manager to contact you for urgent time-sensitive matters.

Fax:

Your fax number may be used if your project manager needs to fax you information.

Email Address:

All system notification is sent to this email address.  It is very important that this email address be accurate, otherwise you may not be notified of available localization jobs.

Locale for Email and Date/Times:

Specify the language of automatic GlobalSight notifications.
