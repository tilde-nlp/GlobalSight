Workflow Status Summary

Summary:

Jobs have a status based on the workflows contained in the job.  If a job has a workflows in more than one of the states listed below, the job will be listed in all of those states.

Pending

Jobs not ready for dispatch

  • Make Ready moves a pending job to the Ready status

  • Discard removes a job from GlobalSight entirely

Ready

Jobs ready to dispatch into the GlobalSight localization process

  • Dispatch moves a job or workflow to the In Progress status, and localization begins

  • Discard removes a job or workflow from GlobalSight entirely

In Progress

Jobs currently in the process of localization

  • Discard removes a job or workflow from GlobalSight entirely

  • You can also modify individual workflows and activities from here

Localized

Jobs with completed workflows (all activities finished)

  • Export initiates the export process from GlobalSight

 

Exported

Jobs with workflows whose pages have successfully exported

  • Re-export allows you to re-export any pages

  • Archive - this option only appears once all workflows have been successfully exported

Archived

Jobs that have been archived

  • Re-export allows you to re-export any pages
