TM Matches

Exact Match - The new source text is identical to text found in the TM.

 

Fuzzy Match - The new source text is similar to the text found in the TM.

 

No Match - No text was found in the TM that matched the new source text.

 

See also No Match Repetitions.

 

Example

If the TM contains the following segments for English (US) to German (Germany).

 

English (US)

German (Germany)

Welcome to Acme Corporation.

Herzlich willkommen bei Acme Corporation.

You can't find better products than ours.

Bessere Produkte sind nicht zu finden.

Thank you for your visit.

Wir danken Ihnen für Ihren Besuch.

 

These new segments would be matched as follows:

 

New Source

Match

Welcome to Acme Corporation.

Exact Match

Herzlich willkommen bei Acme Corporation..

You can't find a better product than ours.

75% match

Bessere Produkte sind nicht zu finden.

Thanks for stopping by.
