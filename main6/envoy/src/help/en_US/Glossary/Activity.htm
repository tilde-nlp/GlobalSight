Activity

A step, described by the activity type, in the localization process for a workflow in a job.  The content to be localized is assigned to a specific user or all qualified users, and has a time to accept and time to complete.

 

Example:

 

Job Name: First Group

Activity Type: Translation

Workflow: German (Germany)

Content to localize: index.htm, toc.htm, intro.htm, welcome.htm

User: Heinrich Schmidt

Time to Accept: 4 hours

Time to Complete: 2 days
