Job

A job is a collection of workflows (each specific to a target locale) that all have the same content for localization.  The workflows in a Job are based on the workflow templates in the localization profile that was selected at time of import.

 

Example:

Job Name: First Group

Workflows: German (Germany), French (France), Spanish (Mexico), Spanish (Spain).

Content to localize: index.htm, toc.htm, intro.htm, welcome.htm
